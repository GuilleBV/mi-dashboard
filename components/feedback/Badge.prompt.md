Status pill for labels, priorities, streak/category tags. Tone maps to the semantic palette; add `dot` for a status indicator, `solid` for high-emphasis.

```jsx
<Badge tone="green" dot>Completado</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" solid>Vence en 2 días</Badge>
<Badge tone="blue" size="sm">Gym</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral` · `blue` · `green` · `amber` · `danger`.
